Our Story

Connecting the Lab to Real Patient Care

The Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: that the gap between laboratory discovery and clinical application costs patients time, and sometimes lives.

Our work is driven by a commitment to bridging laboratory insights with real-world clinical decisions. We study rare hematologic diseases ‚Äî conditions where early identification and timely intervention are critical ‚Äî and develop the tools and evidence needed to act faster and smarter at the bedside.

We are equally committed to training the next generation of physician-scientists in a collaborative environment where curiosity drives discovery ‚Äî and discovery improves lives.
